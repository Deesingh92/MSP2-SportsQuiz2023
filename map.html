The interactive map showcases the top ten largest stadiums in the world by capacity, elegantly displayed as marker clusters. Each cluster represents a group of stadiums in close proximity, simplifying the viewing experience. With a quick glance, users can identify the locations of iconic venues like the Narendra Modi Stadium in India, Rungrado May Day Stadium in North Korea, and Michigan Stadium in the USA, among others. Upon clicking a marker, a bit of information about including their names and seating capacities. Whether you're a sports enthusiast or simply curious about these monumental structures, this map offers an engaging and informative exploration of the world's largest stadiums.
